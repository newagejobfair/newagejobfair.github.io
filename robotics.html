Robotics Companies

Robotics

Many different robotics companies will be present to take questions and talk about opportunities. These companies will pitch their futuristic ideas and products with hopes to expand their workforce. The lack of qualified technical personal is an ongoing challenge for robotics and intelligent systems companies, as well as a key gating factor for the continued expansion of the robotics sector. Please come ready to talk with many professionals and researchers in the robotics industry.


Robots4Society is a major tech company that strives to improve society through the field of robotics. They undertake challengeing problems and produce many impressive and intelligence solutions to these problems. With their new government contract to help produce rockets for space, they are reaching out to students for help. Good option for students who are looking for:

  • Job Security
  • Good Benefits package
  • Remote
  • Training Provided

Hackedt is a student-run company whose mission statement is to better publish hackathon project ideas and help students bring those ideas into the real world. This company is only a startup and needs a strong workforce to help build it up. This is a great company to get lots of experience from. Good for students who want:

  • An opportunity to develop and apply skills
  • Stock in a company + sign-on bonus
  • Hybrid
  • Training Provided
