For High Schoolers. By High Schoolers.

New Age Job Fair was made by high schoolers in the Northern Virginia Area after the COVID-19 Pandemic started. We knew that high schoolers heavily rely on job and career fairs to find opportunities and they would be missing out this spring. To solve this, we got together and contacted hundreds of companies within our area and we organized this fair for our peers.

How it works

Throughout the day, students will be able to attend zoom meetings to directly talk with recruiters from top companies. Recruiters will briefly go over relevant information pertaining to positions being offered. Students will be able to ask recruiters questions and be able to network with them. The timing of the meeting will be posted the day before and the students will be able to book which meetings they want to go to.
